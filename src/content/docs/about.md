# Your data. Your devices. Your AI.

Every time you use cloud services, your data flows through servers owned by tech giants. Your documents, photos, messages, and queries are all processed by their infrastructure. They see everything. You're not the customer – you're the product.

**Cloudless Computer** teaches you to run software and AI on your own hardware. No data leaves your devices. No algorithms learn from your behavior. No one profits from your thoughts.

Your digital life should belong to you.

___

## Our Beliefs

While most tech companies profit from your attention and data, I believe you deserve tools that respect your privacy and put you in control.

We believe:

-   **Your data is your property** – What you create, ask, and communicate should never be mined, analyzed, or sold.
-   **Ownership beats convenience** – True security comes from running software on infrastructure you control, not rented servers you don't.
-   **AI should serve you** – Large language models and AI tools should work for your benefit, not as data collection mechanisms for corporations.
-   **The alternative exists** – Powerful, private AI and software run locally. You don't have to trade privacy for capability.
-   **Small steps compound** – Building a cloudless workflow isn't about perfection; it's about progress and reclaiming more of your digital life over time.

With this approach, Cloudless Computer has become a resource for creators, professionals, and privacy-conscious individuals who want to break free from Big Tech dependency.

___

## Our Approach

I believe the only reliable path to digital sovereignty is through observation, experimentation, and iteration. Unlike shortcuts or one-size-fits-all solutions, this process helps you:

-   **Observe** the current landscape of cloudless tools and technologies.
-   **Discover** your unique path through experimentation with local-first software.
-   **Build** a workflow that respects your privacy without sacrificing productivity.
-   **Unlock** the power of AI that works entirely on your device.

We provide more than education – you get practical guides, tested configurations, and ongoing support to help you achieve lasting digital independence.

Hi, I'm Daniel – creator of Cloudless Computer.

A few years ago, I realized how dependent I had become on cloud services for everything: documents, photos, AI assistants, note-taking, even my calendar. Each service promised convenience, but the hidden cost was my data – flowing through servers I didn't control, being processed by algorithms I couldn't see.

I started exploring alternatives. Small steps at first: a local note-taking app here, a self-hosted calendar there. Then I discovered local AI models and realized that even large language models could run entirely on my own hardware.

The more I experimented, the more I realized how much was possible. Today, I run nearly my entire digital life offline – AI assistants, document processing, image generation, all on devices I own. My data never leaves my house.

We're here to help you do the same.
